Delete Your Account

You can request complete deletion of your Glide account and all associated data stored on our servers. Here's how.

1

Send us an email

Email us at utsavmdesai@gmail.com with the subject line "Delete My Glide Account".

2

Include your account details

In the body of the email, include the email address associated with your Google Sign-In so we can locate your account.

3

We process your request

We will verify your identity and permanently delete all your data from our Firebase servers within 30 days of receiving your request. You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion is complete.

What happens to your data

Data Type Action Timeline
Firebase account & authentication Deleted Within 30 days
Game progress (coins, unlocked planes, equipped plane) Deleted Within 30 days
High score (cloud) Deleted Within 30 days
Leaderboard entry (display name + score) Removed Within 30 days
Local device data (SharedPreferences) Kept on device Deleted when you uninstall the app

Important Notes

  • Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. All cloud-saved progress, coins, and unlocked planes will be lost.
  • If you played as a Guest, no account exists on our servers. Simply uninstall the app to remove all local data.
  • To revoke Google Sign-In permissions independently, visit your Google Account Permissions page.
